# 🔹 STEP 3 – RAPID REINFORCEMENT

## 🔐 Identity Fix

- RBAC = WHO  
- Policy = WHAT  

---

## 🌐 Networking Fix

- NSG = filter  
- UDR = route  
- Peering ≠ transitive  

---

## 📦 Storage Fix

- Blob vs File vs Disk  
- RBAC > SAS > Keys  
- GRS vs ZRS  

---

## 🖥️ Compute Fix

- VM vs App Service  
- Set vs Zone  
- VMSS = scale  

---

## 📊 Monitoring Fix

- Metrics vs Logs  
- Log Analytics required  

---

## 📜 Governance Fix

- Policy effects  
- Budget = alert only

# 🔹 STEP 5 – EXAM STRATEGY

## 🧠 Question Strategy

1. Read LAST sentence first  
2. Identify keyword:
   - Secure → Private Endpoint
   - Enforce → Policy
   - Route → UDR
   - Scale → VMSS/App Service

---

## 🔥 Elimination Strategy

Remove:
- Irrelevant services
- Over-complicated answers

---

## ⏱️ Time Strategy

- 1 min per question
- Flag difficult ones
- Return later

---

## ⚠️ Watch For

- “BEST solution”
- “MINIMUM effort”
- “LOWEST cost”

👉 These change the answer
