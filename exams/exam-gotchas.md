# AZ-104 Exam Gotchas

- Policy enforces configuration, RBAC does not.
- Locks can block deletion even for powerful users.
- Contributor cannot assign roles.
- Peering is not transitive.
- Lower NSG priority number wins.
- Storage redundancy answers depend on scenario wording.
- Availability sets and zones are not interchangeable.
- Tags organize resources but do not enforce compliance by themselves.
